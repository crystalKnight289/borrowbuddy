About BorrowBuddy

BorrowBuddy is a peer-to-peer rental platform that allows individuals to rent out their unused items and borrow what they need from others nearby. We're building a circular, cost-effective economy where resources are reused, not wasted — strengthening community and sustainability.

How to Borrow an Item

Follow these simple steps to borrow an item from our community:

Step Description
1. Browse Listings Visit the Borrow page to view items available in your area.
2. Select Item Click on an item listing to view its details and contact the owner.
3. Request & Coordinate Message the owner and agree on pickup/drop-off logistics.

How to Rent Your Item

Got something lying around? Here's how to start earning by sharing:

Step Description
1. Create an Account Sign up or log in to your BorrowBuddy profile.
2. List Your Item Provide title, description, pricing, images, and contact info.
3. Publish & Manage Post the listing and respond to inquiries through your dashboard.

How to Sell an Item

Want to permanently give away an item instead of renting it? We’ve got you:

Step Description
1. Visit Sell Page Click on the “Rent Your Item” button from the homepage.
2. Choose Sale Option Select the option for permanent sale (if enabled by platform settings).
3. Complete Form Add all necessary details and publish your item for sale.
